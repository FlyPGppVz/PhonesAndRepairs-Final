Privacy & Transparency

Cookie Settings & Policy

At CellphonesAndRepair, we believe in radical transparency. We use cookies to enhance your technical journey, remembering your preferences and ensuring your secure repair requests remain uninterrupted.

cookie

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our digital workshop. They act as a memory for the website, allowing us to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

They help us deliver a smoother browsing experience, such as keeping your device repair status visible across pages or maintaining your shopping cart items as you browse our component selection.
